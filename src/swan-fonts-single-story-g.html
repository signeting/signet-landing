Serif fonts (double-story g): More traditional elegance, higher stroke contrast, more swan-like curves

Sans-serif fonts (single-story g): More modern, cleaner at small sizes, better screen rendering, but less dramatic swan-neck aesthetic

üí° Recommendation:

For the favicon, consider using Raleway or Montserrat at weight 700-800. They maintain elegance while the single-story "g" keeps things clean and legible at tiny sizes.

For the main landing page animation, you could still use a serif font like Playfair Display for maximum drama, and reserve the single-story g font for body text or secondary uses.
